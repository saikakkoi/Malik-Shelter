## What is Malik Shelter?

Malik Shelter is a breeding entity that provides adoption services. By leveraging comprehensive animal identity data—including personality traits and medical history—it ensures adopters find pets that truly match their lifestyle and preferences, fostering longer-lasting and happier bonds.

### The Problem We Solve

| Challenge | Impact |
| POOR_MATCHING | Adopters often choose pets based on looks alone, leading to potential returns or unhappy homes. |
| INFO_ASYMMETRY | Critical medical and personality data is often scattered or missing at the point of adoption. |
| SEARCH_FRICTION | Finding a pet with specific characteristics is time-consuming. |

### Our Solution

We provide a centralized, data-rich adoption portal where every pet profile is complete with medical records and personality assessments, enabling precise filtering and matching for adopters.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1️⃣ Advanced Discovery
- Detailed filtering by species, breed, age, size, and gender.
- **Personality matching** based on behavioral tags (e.g., "Good with kids," "High energy").


### 2️⃣ Transparency & Trust
- **Full Medical History:** Access to vaccination, spay/neuter status, and known health issues.
- **Personality Profiles:** insights into temperament and compatibility.

### 3️⃣ Adoption Enablement
- Streamlined inquiry process.
- Favorites and simplified comparison tools.
